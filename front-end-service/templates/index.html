Background

In weeks 4 & 5 of 10 Academy trainings of batch 4, We built an AI model for either Swihali or Amharic language. For both of the cases, We have experienced the lack and quality of data. If we Had had a diverse and large training set, our model could have improved and our model could have transformed the lives of many in East Africa.

Recognizing the value of large data sets for speech-to-text data sets, and seeing the opportunity that there are many text corpuses for both languages, and understanding that complex data engineering skills is valuable. That is why we designed and built a robust, large scale, fault tolerant, highly available Kafka cluster that can be used to post a sentence and receive an audio file.
